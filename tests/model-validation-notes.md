# Model Validation Notes

Suggested validation tasks:

- Compare revenue output against official revenue anchors.
- Compare budget envelope against official expenditure anchors.
- Compare debt/GDP against Bureau of the Treasury data.
- Compare remittance values against BSP cash remittance data.
- Keep complete annual rows separate from partial-year or projected rows.
- Run backtests after importing evidence-grade data.
- Record model version, data version, and assumptions used in each published scenario.
